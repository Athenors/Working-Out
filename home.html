Working Out

What is working out and why should you start?

Working out is a practice or exercise to test or improve one's fitness for athletic competition, ability, or performance. Working out is very beneficial to health to people of all ages and is encouraged worldwide. Its massive amount of benefits with no negatives pushes people to be the best they can be each day!

Health benefits of working out

Benefits Include:
  1. Aids in managing weight
  2. Reduce risk of disease
  3. Strengthen bones and muscles
  4. Improve your memory and brain function
  5. Reduce anxiety and depression

Different Types of working out inclue:

  • Bodybuilding
  • Powerlifting
  • Cardio
  • Calisthenics
  • For Fun!

Tips and Short Video

  1. Listen to Music
  2. Follow a program or workout plan
  3. Drink LOTS of water
  4. Make sure you get adequate sleep
  5. Dont do just cardio, Lift some weights too!
